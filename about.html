Fresh Clothes, Fresh Perspective

We don’t just wash clothes; we give you your weekends back.

Founded in 2022, Lather & Loft began with a simple observation: life is too short to spend it in a basement waiting for a dryer to beep. We set out to create a laundry experience that feels less like a chore and more like a luxury.

Eco-Friendly

Expert Care

From delicate silks to heavy-duty denim, we treat every fabric with surgical precision.

Fast Turnaround
